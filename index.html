About us

Welcome to The Wicked Book Club, where the joy of reading transforms into a shared journey among friends! Born out of a collective desire to cultivate better habits and incorporate more reading into our daily lives, our club brings together avid readers from across San Francisco every month to dive into a new book.

Our meetings are the heart of our club. Held every Sunday, these sessions are a time for reflection, lively discussion, and learning from the diverse perspectives of our members. Each meeting is an opportunity to explore the themes, narratives, and lessons of our monthly book selection. Whether itâ€™s an inspiring biography, a gripping novel, or a thought-provoking non-fiction piece, every book is a gateway to expanding our horizons.

The Wicked Book Club is not just about reading; it's about building a community. While we embrace the convenience of virtual meetings, we also cherish our monthly in-person gatherings at a local coffee shop in San Francisco. These face-to-face interactions strengthen our bonds and reaffirm our commitment to each other and the habit of reading.

Join us in this literary adventure, where each page turned is a step towards personal growth and collective enlightenment.
